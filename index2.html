Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to make an appointment?

While walk-ins are welcome, we highly recommend making an appointment to ensure minimal waiting time and to guarantee that you'll be seen by a doctor.

+

What insurance plans do you accept?

We accept most major insurance plans. Please check with our receptionist or your insurance provider for confirmation.

+

How can I pay for my medical expenses?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and most insurance plans. Payment is expected at the time of service unless other arrangements have been made in advance.

+

Do you offer telemedicine services?

Yes, we offer telemedicine appointments for certain medical conditions. Please contact our office to schedule a telemedicine appointment.

+

Can I request a prescription refill online?

Yes, you can request a prescription refill by contacting our office
